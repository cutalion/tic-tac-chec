How to play →

First color with four pieces in a row wins.

  • Play on a 4×4 board. Each player has four pieces: pawn, rook, bishop, knight.
  • On your turn, place a piece from your hand onto any empty square, or move one already on the board.
  • You only control pieces of your color.
  • Pieces move like their chess counterparts.
  • Capturing returns the captured piece to its owner's hand.
  • Pawns reverse direction when they reach the far edge.
